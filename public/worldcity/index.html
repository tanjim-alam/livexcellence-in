Project Overview

Sobha World City is a premium international-style residential township by Sobha Limited, located near Hoskote, East Bangalore. The project offers thoughtfully designed luxury homes set amidst lush green landscapes, along with world-class amenities for a modern and comfortable lifestyle.
Hoskote is a fast-developing residential and industrial hub, known for its excellent connectivity to Whitefield, IT corridors, and key areas of Bangalore via NH-75. With upcoming infrastructure developments, reputed schools, healthcare facilities, and growing commercial zones, Hoskote is emerging as a preferred destination for homebuyers and investors seeking long-term value and peaceful living close to the city.
